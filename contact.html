Important information

The garden, welcomes everybody between 10am - 4pm from monday to sunday, it's easily accesable for everyone. Unfortunately, we cannot provide parking facility, but 23 and 27 bus from Hannover Street takes you directly to the enterance.

We kindly offer our cafe & restaurant services, with wide variety choices of local pastries, cakes, coffees and delicous meals.

We've just introduced our "Garden Rooms" hotel services, with that you're able to spend your night in one of the world's most beautiful garden.

Some of our parts are unfortunately closed, in order to protect the safety of our plants.

Before visit please make sure, you're familier with the Scottish Goverment's current COVID-19 regulations

Regarding to the COVID-19, we kindly ask our visitors, to book a ticket in advance, and also to wear face covering while staying.
